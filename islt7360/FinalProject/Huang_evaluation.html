Summary of Evaluatiors' Comments:

  • Summary:

    Accroding to the feedback, I found that people with different background will provide suggestions on different parts. Use Cases:

The purpose of my website is to introduce my father as an amateur photographer and share his works and stories. Based on this purpose, I was proposed three use cases: creating three different html files to introduce three different parts; sharing photographic works through collaging images on portfolio webpage and link all the photos to the Google Photo; using a photo which related to the story as the background on the story webpage. After the user testing, I concluded the feedbacks on these use cases:

  • All the evaluators are satisfied with the layout and the navigation. They could easily visit all the webpages, and tell the different parts clearly. But since I added target = "_blank" function, the evaluators felt frustrated to keep closing new opening pages.

  • They are also like the collaging layout of portfolio webpage. But there are still suggestions: the evaluators who are interested in photography would like to know more information about the photographic works. And it's questionable to link photos to Google Photo.

  • The background photo is successfully made the webpage more eye-catching. The evaluators are impressived on this.

Recommended/Planned Changes to the Design:

Based on the feedbacks, I would like to do the following changes on my design:

  • Since Yidong mentioned that he would like to get the information about photos, I would like to add a feature that when the user hover over a photo, it will show the place that this photo was taken.

  • I think what Yidong said about linking to Google Photo is reasonable. So I would like to delete this feature.

  • I would like to change the header of the homepage. Because Susan mentioned that it could be confused with portfolio page.

  • Also, evaluators felt inconvenient to keep closing new opening webpages, I will delete the target = "_blank" function in the navigation bar.

  • Accordingn to Dorothy's suggestion,I would like to add another panel on the story webpage, to introduce some details about the story.
