My father always believes that the pasion is the most important thing of doing everything, like photography. He would like to consider awards as the approval of such pasion. Although he has obtained some awards in the past years, his goal is still photography itself. Here, will introduce some of his awards.

2016 CHINESE ART GOLDEN HORSE AWARD CANDIDATE

First time to be the candidate of this award

The Golden Horse Award of Chinese Art is to honor the artists who have made outstanding achievements in the field of Chinese art. The winners must first win the highest prize in the authoritative art activities and competitions at home and abroad before they have the basic qualifications to apply for the Golden Horse Award of Chinese Art. My father was honored to be one of a hundrend candidates of this award. This was his very first time to get involed in a grand competition with many prominent photographers.

2017 ART GOLDEN HORSE AWARD

TROPHY WINNER

In 2017, my father had a significant break. His work "the Old Woman and the Sheep", which won the PSA gold medal of the Color Open Group at the second Black Mountain ZETA International Photography Exhibition in 2017, gained the chinese art golden horse award.
